    About BikeParkLondon

    BikeParkLondon helps you find solo motorcycle parking bays across London. Please read the notes below before relying on this map.

    Experimental site

    This is an experimental project provided for information only. Coverage, pricing, and bay availability may be wrong or out of date. Always check on-street signage and official council guidance before parking.

    Parking bay data

    Bays shown on the map come from OpenStreetMap across the UK, queried weekly via the Overpass API. London borough rules below apply only to bays inside London; coverage is crowdsourced and may be incomplete in some areas.

    Borough rules

    Policy summaries in the details panel and borough directory are compiled from publicly available council parking guidance. They are general summaries, not legal advice, and may not reflect recent tariff or emissions-based changes.

    Map & search

    • Base map: CartoDB Voyager tiles (© OpenStreetMap contributors, © CARTO)
    • Search & addresses: Nominatim / OpenStreetMap geocoding
    • Directions: Google Maps (external link)
    • Analytics: Privacy-friendly usage stats via Cloudflare (page views and coarse interaction counts — no postcodes or exact locations).
